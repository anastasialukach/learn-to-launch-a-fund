# Learn to Launch a Fund — Research & Structure

## Logical Blocks (Draft Structure)

### 1. Fund Basics & Legal Foundations
- Fund structures (LP/GP, SPV, rolling funds, syndicates)
- Legal entity formation (Delaware LP, Cayman, offshore structures)
- SEC registration (exempt reporting advisers, RIA)
- Compliance & regulatory requirements
- Fund documents (LPA, side letters, subscription docs)

### 2. Fundraising from LPs
- LP types & motivations (institutions, HNWIs, family offices, endowments, pensions)
- First-time fund challenges
- Track record building (angel investing, SPVs, scout programs)
- Pitch deck structure for GPs
- Data room preparation
- First close vs final close strategy
- Commitment letters & soft circles
- Anti-dilution provisions

### 3. Fund Economics & Terms
- 2/20 model (and variations: 1/15, 1.5/18, 2.5/25)
- Management fee calculation (committed capital vs deployed capital)
- Carried interest & waterfall structures (American vs European)
- Preferred return (hurdle rate)
- Clawback provisions
- GP commit requirements
- Key person clauses

### 4. Fund Operations & Infrastructure
- Fund administrator selection
- Legal counsel (formation, ongoing)
- Accounting & tax (K-1s, UBTI)
- Banking & custody
- Cap table management tools
- LP portal & reporting systems
- Insurance (D&O, E&O)

### 5. Investment Strategy & Thesis
- Thesis development (sector, stage, geography, check size)
- Portfolio construction (concentration vs diversification)
- Reserve strategy (pro-rata rights, follow-on allocation)
- Ownership targets
- Exit timeline assumptions
- Power law dynamics

### 6. Deal Flow & Sourcing
- Proprietary deal flow vs competitive rounds
- Network effects & positioning
- Warm intro strategies
- Outbound sourcing
- Platform value-add (recruiting, BD, technical)
- Co-investment partners

### 7. Due Diligence & Investment Process
- DD framework (market, product, team, traction, unit economics)
- Investment memo templates
- IC process & decision-making
- Term sheet negotiation
- Legal DD & closing
- Reference checks

### 8. Portfolio Management & Value Creation
- Board participation
- Value-add playbooks
- Follow-on decision framework
- Down-round scenarios
- Exit planning & secondary sales
- Liquidity events (IPO, M&A, tender offers)

### 9. LP Relations & Reporting
- Quarterly LP letters
- Annual meetings
- NAV calculations & fair value
- TVPI, DPI, RVPI metrics
- Fund performance attribution
- Re-up strategy for Fund II

### 10. GP Brand & Positioning
- Thought leadership & content
- Conference speaking
- Media relations
- Social presence (LinkedIn, X)
- Newsletter/Substack
- Podcast guesting
